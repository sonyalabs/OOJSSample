Employee Management Portal

It is an internal company homepage where employees and HR team can access all HR related information. Employees can apply leave and clarify or complain issues related to HR

Main Fetures are:

  • Create new employee profile and enter company and personal information Create a new Employee
  • Display Employee details Display Employee List
  • Update employee details as it changes Modify Employee Detail
  • Remove Employees from the list Remove Employees from list
